## Manoa Gym Ranking
Proposers: Dylan Dela Cruz, Allen Long

## Overview
The Problem : Students throughout the semester focus too much on their academics and often neglect their physical well-being.

The Solution : To encourage more students to be more active, Manoa GymRanking is designed to show and compare each students' best records of their physical abilities.

## Mockup Page Ideas
- Login Portal
- Landing Page
- Leaderboards Homepage (For Each Category)
- Submission page (video evidence)
- Forum page
- Admin Verification Page

## Use Case Ideas
- UHM Students would login with their UH Credentials and be brought to the leaderboard homepage
- User would submit video evidence of their accomplishment
- Administrators review and verify video evidence, manage leaderboards, respond to end user about the legitimacy of their submission

## Beyond The Basics
- live gym status
- Statistics page (ex. total weight lifted amongst all users)
- exercise tracker
